## CONTENT FUNDAMENTALS

### Language
- **Primary language: Brazilian Portuguese.** All product copy, UI labels, and marketing text is in pt-BR.
- Dates use Brazilian convention: "Abril 2026"

### Tone &amp; Voice
- **Direct, factual, data-first.** Claims are immediately backed by numbers (ANVISA 2024, McKinsey 2023, DROME Research).
- **Professional but not bureaucratic.** No corporate filler. Short sentences. Confident.
- **Action-oriented.** Every section ends in a clear call to action ("Agendar demo", "Ver plataforma").
- **No emoji.** Zero emoji anywhere in UI or copy. Data symbols (°C, ₂, R$) are used precisely.
- **Numbers are heroes.** Big stats are the primary visual motif in both marketing and product.

### Copy Patterns
- Eyebrows label sections in uppercase mono: `HOSPITAL INTELLIGENCE`, `O PROBLEMA`, `PLATAFORMA`
- Headlines avoid fluff: "Previna violações antes que aconteçam" (direct imperative)
- Metrics always cite their source: "ANVISA · 2024", "ABNT · 2023", "DROME Research"
- CTAs use action verbs: "Agendar demo", "Ver plataforma", "Ver documentação"
- Technical specs written precisely: "0,3°C/min", "91% confiança", "RDC 430", "FDA 21 CFR"

### Casing
- Product names: Title Case (Cadeia de Frio, Gases Medicinais)
- Eyebrows: ALL CAPS (rendered via CSS `text-transform: uppercase`)
- UI labels: Sentence case
- Compliance codes: Uppercase abbreviations (ANVISA, FDA, LGPD, RTLS)

### Writing "I vs You"
- The brand writes in **second person** to the buyer: "sua equipe", "seu ambiente", "você precisa"

## VISUAL FOUNDATIONS

### Colors
- **Brand blue `#3B6FA8`** — derived from the triangle logo mark. Used as accent, CTA, eyebrows, data highlights. Navy-steel, not electric. Confident, clinical, trustworthy.
- **Ink scale** — warm navy-gray (not cool blue-gray). `--ink-900: #0F1823` is the dark background. 10-step scale from near-black to near-white.
- **Data palette** — 5 semantic colors for status: emerald (OK), amber (warning/drift/prediction), rose (alarm/critical), cyan (info), violet (RTLS/location).
- **No decorative gradients.** The only gradient is the accent radial in mega-menu featured cards and the subtle `linear-gradient(180deg,#FFF1F2,#fff)` on alert KPI cards.

### Typography
- **Inter Tight** — display/headings. Tight tracking (−0.035em on h1). Weight 650 for headlines (OpenType fractional weight, not just 700). Very compact leading (1.04 on h1).
- **Inter** — body, UI, labels. Weight 400–600.
- **JetBrains Mono** — eyebrows, badges, source citations, timestamps, technical labels, mono data. Used extensively as a "technical credibility" signal.
- All fonts loaded via Google Fonts (no self-hosted files provided).

### Spacing &amp; Layout
- Container: 1280px max, 24px gutter
- Section vertical rhythm: 120px (tight: 72px)
- Card padding: 28px
- Grid: 2–6 columns, always 24px gap

### Backgrounds &amp; Surfaces
- **Light mode default:** `#FFFFFF` background, `#F7F9FC` subtle, no texture
- **Dark panels:** `#0F1823` (ink-900). Used for hero sections, slide covers, sidebar, problem sections. Full sections flip dark, not individual cards.
- **Slide system:** White slides + `#F7F9FC` alternates + dark (`#0F1823`) for covers/dividers
- **No background images or illustrations.** Visual interest comes from data, SVG diagrams, and typography scale.
- **Placeholder areas** use `repeating-linear-gradient(45deg, transparent 0 8px, rgba(...) 8px 9px)` — a subtle cross-hatch pattern.

### Corner Radii
- sm: 4px (tags, badges tight)
- md: 6px (tags normal, small buttons)
- lg: 8px (buttons default)
- xl: 12px (cards)
- 2xl: 14px (modals, panels)
- 3xl: 16px (large panels)
- full: 99px (pills, badges rounded)

### Shadows
Four-level shadow system, all using `rgba(15, 24, 35, …)` (the ink-900 color):
- **shadow-1**: Default card (barely visible)
- **shadow-2**: Card hover / elevated
- **shadow-3**: Dropdowns / floating elements
- **shadow-4**: Modals / heavy overlays
- **shadow-focus**: 3px focus ring using `color-mix(accent 25%, transparent)`

### Borders
- Default: `1px solid #E5E7EB` (ink-200)
- Strong: `1px solid #D1D5DB` (ink-300)
- Dark panels: `1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.08)` / `0.16` strong

### Animations
- **Micro (160ms ease):** hover and press transitions
- **Enter (200ms ease-out):** fast entrances (nav mega-menu)
- **Reveal (700ms ease):** scroll-reveal `.reveal` → `.reveal.in`
- **Spring (420ms cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1)):** panel opens, modals
- **Pulse dot:** eyebrow decorative dot pulses at 2s ease-in-out (scale 1→1.25)
- **Ticker:** logo track scrolls infinitely at 70s (slow, hypnotic)
- **No bounce, no overshoot on data.** Spring only on structural elements.

### Hover &amp; Press States
- Buttons: `translateY(-1px)` lift + darker background
- Nav links: subtle `background: color-mix(fg 5%, transparent)` fill
- Cards (vision nodes): `translateY(-4px)` lift + accent border + glow shadow
- Logo ticker: `grayscale(0)` reveal on hover (default is `grayscale(1) opacity(0.7)`)
- Ghost buttons on dark: `rgba(255,255,255,0.06)` fill on hover

### Cards
- White background, `1px solid #E5E7EB`, 12px radius, `shadow-1` default
- On hover: `shadow-2` (subtle lift in shadow, often paired with translateY)
- Dark panel cards: `rgba(255,255,255,0.04)` bg, `rgba(255,255,255,0.08)` border

### Imagery &amp; Iconography
- **No photography.** No illustrations. No icons library.
- SVG diagrams and data visualizations only (charts, flow diagrams)
- Client logos in ticker: grayscale + 70% opacity by default
- Hero visual: placeholder for animated isometric dashboard (not implemented)

### Slide System
- 1920×1080px, scaled by deck-stage.js
- Two background modes: white/light-gray and dark `#0F1823`
- Brand accent stripe: right-side diagonal clip-path polygon at `#3B6FA8`

- Footer bar: 6px solid `#3B6FA8` at bottom of all light slides

### Color Vibe of Imagery
